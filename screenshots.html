Multi-Texturing

Multiple diffuse texture are used to generate the final diffuse map. At the bottom a sand texture is used and at the top a snow texture. For step an additional rock texture is in use, which will blend it to the other textures. And at least the grass texture will be taken for flat grounds

Specular Light

Additional to ambient and diffuse light there is specular light for improved realism. The shininess of the terrain should be low otherwise it gets a touch of plastic as shown in the graphic.
